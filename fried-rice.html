Breakfast Fried Rice!

Fried rice, filled with savory vegetables and sauteed meats, and topped with a crispy fried egg, is a delicious combination of healthy carbs, protein, and fat. Plus, gut-friendly fiber without being overwhelming. Soy sauce, sesame oil, and a few green onions to garnish pull together a really simple dish with complex flavor. Get creative and try adding potatoes, salsa, and green chili for a Southwestern spin.
