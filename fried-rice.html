Breakfast Fried Rice!

Fluffy rice mixed with savory vegetables and sauteed meats, and then topped with a crispy fried egg, is a delicious combination of healthy carbs, protein, and fat. Soy sauce, sesame oil, and a few green onions to garnish pull together a really simple dish with complex flavor. Get creative and try adding potatoes, salsa, and green chili for a Southwestern spin.
